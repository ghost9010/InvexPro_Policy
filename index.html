Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to provide, improve, and secure our services. The categories of data we collect are outlined below.

A. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Application, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Device Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage statistics and screens visited
  • Time, date, and duration of sessions
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data for performance improvement

This data helps us maintain stability and deliver a seamless user experience.

B. User-Provided Information

To access certain features, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Email address (business or personal contact)
  • Business or store name
  • Product catalog and inventory data
  • Transaction and sales records
  • Backup or export files generated within the app

This information is used solely to provide core stock management features and related services.

C. Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time GPS or location data from your device.

D. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

InvexPro does not employ Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process, analyse, or infer personal user data.
